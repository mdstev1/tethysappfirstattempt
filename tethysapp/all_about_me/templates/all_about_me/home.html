About Me




My name is Michael. I love basketball, food, and my family. This page gives a brief introduction to me.




I am from Colorado Springs, CO, home of Pike's Peak, the Air Force Academy, and lots of outdoor adventures. I grew up there from age 7 until I left for college.

I come from a family of 7 kids: I have 2 brothers and 4 sisters. 2 of my sisters have gotten married in the past year, with a third getting married this May.

I have been at BYU since 2013, but served a mission in Pennsylvania from 2014-2016. My mission was life-changing, and I feel its effects every single day.

I met my wife through some friends from high school (yes, set-ups do sometimes work!). We were married in 2017 and enjoy sports, hiking, traveling, and watching movies.
